What is this?

Resume Manager allows you to quickly, and easily upload a resume to let potential employers contact you if they believe you are a good fit for the job. We're already in a paperless world, whats the need for having to print out a resume, when some of us don't even have printers? Upload your resume here and have access to it on any device, anywhere, and anytime. Simply search up your resume with the 6 character code generated by our service.
